# Assumptions and Open Questions

## Current Assumptions (Membership Applications Slice)
1. We will model **two core use cases** only, and use alternate flows to cover sub-scenarios.
2. Application outcomes are: **Accepted, Denied, On-Hold, Waitlisted**.
3. Sponsor eligibility validation is performed during submission or committee review.
4. Account creation only occurs when an application is accepted.

## Open Questions
1. Is applicant self-service submission required in phase 1, or staff-assisted entry acceptable?
2. Are digital sponsor signatures required, or just sponsor identity references?
3. Is committee review strictly monthly in-system, or can it be an operational schedule outside the system?
4. Should applicant notifications be in scope now, or deferred to a later slice?
